## Keywords excluded (~22 of 80)

- Branded head terms with KD > 60 (e.g., "strapi", "sanity") — captured organically by the vendor comparison spokes; not standalone targets.
- Long-tail terms with volume < 100 (e.g., "headless cms api authentication" — kept for Cluster 1 H3 anchor but not a standalone target).
- Branded comparison pages where the volume is concentrated on one vendor only (e.g., "contentful vs prismic" at 110/mo — borderline; included as a minor Cluster 4 spoke option).

## Filter rationale

- Min volume 100/mo — anything below this doesn't justify a standalone page in a single-quarter content plan.
- Max KD 60 — anything above this requires >12 months of E-E-A-T building + backlinks; not a near-term play. (Notably, the head term "headless cms" at KD 55 is the upper bound; it's tractable because the buyer has high topical authority by virtue of being a CMS vendor or developer-tool publisher.)
- Branded terms excluded from informational clusters because the buyer wants topical authority, not branded SoV.
